📋 Notes & Explanations ▼
Property Data – Explanation
- HOA payment: considered an advantage for the neighborhood despite the ongoing cost. It ensures maintenance, enforcement, and upkeep of the community's appearance. It raises demand and preserves a percentage of owner-occupants. Neighborhoods without HOA tend to deteriorate over time.
- Over the past 5 years, the average annual appreciation in similar master-planned communities in the Houston metro area was around 5.4%. This does not predict future performance, but given current market conditions and attractive prices, we present it as a forward-looking estimate. The sensitivity table at the bottom examines expected annual ROI based on different appreciation scenarios.
- In Texas, property tax rates vary by neighborhood and are determined by many factors. Property tax in the early years of a community is set in advance and is expected to be derived from the purchase price.
- Score assigned via AI systems – a comprehensive review of the specific community by property type. Metrics reviewed: area appreciation potential, rental demand (vacancy risk), school district quality, infrastructure, and accessibility.
Financial Data – Explanation
- The amount required as equity to complete the transaction – made up of equity to the builder, initial costs including property setup and closing costs.
- The lender requires pre-funding an Escrow account with one year of property tax and one year of insurance. They also require liquid funds in the account for 3–4 months of projected monthly payments in case of vacancy.
Loan – Financing Percentage – The proforma presents a loan at 65% LTV in order to achieve more attractive rates and minimize the risk of negative cash flow. We can examine a higher loan at purchase to improve return metrics and reduce required equity.
- In the U.S., the final interest rate including exit penalties is only known after completing the underwriting process. It's always possible to reduce the annual interest rate by paying more "points" (origination and setup fees) or increasing exit penalties. The goal is to obtain optimal terms and flexibility for future refinancing.
- The loan is structured with an initial interest-only period, followed immediately by a principal + interest period. The goal is for the loan to remain in place until the mortgage is paid off, without needing to return or "balloon" early. A future refinance can reduce the monthly payment.
Revenue, Expense Projections, and Return Metrics – Explanation
- Potential rent was determined based on recently leased comparable homes, reviewed on sites like Rentometer, Zillow, MLS, Redfin, HAR, and others. Rent will of course vary from property to property even among identical units due to market conditions, listing timing, and other factors.
- Insurance varies based on quotes reviewed prior to purchase for this property type. Insurance covers natural disasters, but since this is not a flood zone, it does not include flood coverage (not required by the mortgage). Flood insurance can be added for an additional premium.
- We estimated the vacancy period at 4%. Sometimes a property will have long-term tenants and the figure will improve; other times turnover will be higher. 4% is the average for this property type in the area – there is no guarantee of actual results.
- This is a brand-new builder home with full warranty on all systems and structure. Even so, tenant turnover may require repainting, carpet replacement, or other updates. In the U.S., it's standard to freshen the unit with each new tenant. Unexpected items may arise. Anything that is the tenant's responsibility will be deducted from the security deposit collected at lease start.
- We created a sensitivity analysis table showing annual ROI figures based on different annual appreciation rates. The 5-year historical average was around 5.3%, but it's important to understand the sensitivities (calculation shown at the bottom of each table).
- Reflects the profit on the accumulated cash from the rental period plus the appreciation gain at sale – after deducting selling costs (typically 5–6% brokerage and 1–2% title, so we've included this deduction).
Costs – Explanation
- The fee we receive from the investor covers end-to-end transaction management: reviewing multiple deals, negotiating, coordinating the group, securing special terms (from builders, lenders, and property managers), assisting with entity formation, bank account setup, building an operational infrastructure, coordinating advisors and professionals (lender, realtor/broker, CPA, attorney, title company, architect, and additional consultants), and ongoing support with all relevant parties. Our service is managerial and advisory, focused on leading the process for foreign investors, creating a professional and transparent framework that protects the investor's interests through key handover, engagement with the management company, and ongoing support. Similar to our activity in Israel, the cost of our advisory service is expected to be lower than the various advantages and savings we achieve for investors.
- LLC formation, obtaining an EIN number (similar to a business registration number), and annual filing fees. Payment goes directly to the office and is part of the general costs. The attorney's office is located in Tel Aviv and an advisory call can be scheduled for a nominal fee. The "setup" amount is only relevant for the first property.
Property setup costs, closing costs, loan origination costs, and other fees may vary between properties, loans, and homes. This is an initial estimate intended to be as accurate as possible.
After purchasing the property and before handover, we will conduct a home inspection so that the builder fixes all deficiencies noted in the report. It is recommended to conduct an additional inspection after one year, at the end of the builder's first warranty period.
Key Points & Investment Advantages
The current dollar exchange rate is at a historic low – a significant currency advantage
A developing neighborhood in an area with significant commercial and residential renewal
The area is characterized by high rental demand – the goal was to identify areas where the likelihood of vacancy is very low
Real estate investment may provide protection against inflation
We tried to present the proforma conservatively – reality may improve the numbers
Opportunity to build a property portfolio – pension income ('Trinity model')
Refinancing option opens the possibility of returning capital from the deal, purchasing additional properties, and reducing the interest rate
The proforma ignores rent increases – rising monthly income enables immediate refinancing and improved terms
